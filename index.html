Hummingbird


Description of the buttons:
You can change the color of the hummingbird by pressing the 'colour' button.
If you want to see how the hummingbird flies, click the 'fly' button.
To see moving love story, press the 'kitschy' button.
If you don't like the effect, you can clear everything by pressing the 'clear' button.

You can click on the corners of the canvas as well:
Click on the top right corner to see the hummingbird flying to a flower and drinking the nectar.
Click on the bottom right corner to keep the hummingbird sitting on a branch.
Click on the left corner (top or bottom) to see the hummingbird flying to a cage.
